**Tsuyoshi Hashimoto**,  

a Japanese computer scientist and lecturer at [Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST)](JAIST "JAIST"). He is head of the group developing the [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") program [Tacos](index.php?title=Tacos&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tacos (page does not exist)") <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>, the four times Gold medal winner at [Computer Olympiads](Computer_Olympiad "Computer Olympiad") <a id="cite-note-4" href="#cite-ref-4">[4]</a>.
