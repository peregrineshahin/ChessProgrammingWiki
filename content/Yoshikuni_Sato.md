**Yoshikuni Sato**,  

a Japanese computer scientist at Graduate School of Systems and Information Engineering, Center for Computational Sciences, [University of Tsukuba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tsukuba). His research interests includes [Monte-Carlo Tree Search](Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") applied to [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi"). His paper *A Shogi Program based on Monte-Carlo Tree Search* <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> won the 2010 [ICGA Journal Award](ICGA_Journal#JournalAward "ICGA Journal").
