**Tsan-sheng Hsu**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientist and Research Fellow at the Institute of Information Science at the [Academia Sinica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academia_Sinica), [Taipei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taipei). His research interests concerns [graph theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graph_theory), [data privacy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_privacy), and the design, analysis, implementation and performance evaluation of algorithms <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>. Graph-theoretical techniques were used to efficient construct large [endgame databases](Endgame_Tablebases "Endgame Tablebases") for [Computer Chinese chess](Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess") <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>.
