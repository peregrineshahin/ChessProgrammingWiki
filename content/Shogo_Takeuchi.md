**Shogo Takeuchi**,  

a Japanese computer scientist, lecturer at School of Information, [Kochi University of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kochi_University_of_Technology), and previously postdoc at Graduate School of Frontier Sciences, the [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo). His research interests include performance evaluation, [search](Search "Search") and [learning](Learning "Learning") algorithms in the computer board games of [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi"), [Chess](Chess "Chess"), [Go](Go "Go"), and [Othello](Othello "Othello") <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
