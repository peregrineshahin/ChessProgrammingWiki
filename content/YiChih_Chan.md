**Yi-Chih Chan**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientist at Department of Computer Science and [Information Engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_engineering), [National Chiao Tung University](National_Chiao_Tung_University "National Chiao Tung University"), [Hsinchu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hsinchu). His research interests include computer games, specially [Connect6](Connect6 "Connect6") and a new approach for [proof-number search](Proof-Number_Search "Proof-Number Search"), named Job-level PN (JL-PN) search, where each node is evaluated or expanded by a heavy-weight job, well suited for parallel processing <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>. He is co-author of [gold medal winning](13th_Computer_Olympiad#Connect6 "13th Computer Olympiad") Connect6 program [NCTU6-Lite](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=585).
