 [](https://www.nikhef.nl/generalstorage/tt-news/nieuws/article/nikhef-researcher-jos-vermaseren-receives-prestigious-erc-advanced-grant/) Jos Vermaseren <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> 
**Jos A. M. Vermaseren**,  

a Dutch [theorecial physicist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theoretical_physics) and researcher at the theory group of [Nikhef](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NIKHEF) at [Amsterdam Science Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam_Science_Park). His research interests include [phenomenology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phenomenology_%28particle_physics%29) of [particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Particle_physics), [perturbative calculations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perturbation_theory) and [computer algebra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symbolic_computation), and he is original author of the [open source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_software) <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> symbolic manipulation system [FORM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FORM_%28symbolic_manipulation_system%29) <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>. He holds a 4th [dan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_%28rank%29) in [Go](Go "Go"), has a small art collection and a far going fascination for Japanese food and drinks.
