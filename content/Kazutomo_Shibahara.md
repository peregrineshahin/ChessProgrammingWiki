**Kazutomo Shibahara**,  

a Japanese computer scientist and assistant professor at Department of Computer Science, [Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo_University_of_Agriculture_and_Technology). 
His research interests include [machine learning](Learning "Learning"), and computer games with focus on [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi"). He is co-author of the Shogi program [Mattari Yuuchan](index.php?title=Mattari_Yuuchan&action=edit&redlink=1 "Mattari Yuuchan (page does not exist)") (afternoon), and the [Blokus Duo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blokus#Blokus_Duo.2FTravel_Blokus) program *EN-FIS*.
