**Jiao Wang**,  

a Chinese computer scientist affiliated with the School of Information Science and Engineering, [Northeastern University China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northeastern_University_%28China%29), [Shenyang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shenyang). He is author of the [Chinese Chess](Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess") program [NEUChess](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=114), Bronce medal winner of the [10th Computer Olympiad, Taipei 2005](10th_Computer_Olympiad#ChineseChess "10th Computer Olympiad") and two times Gold medal winner, at the [11th Computer Olympiad, Turin 2006](11th_Computer_Olympiad#ChineseChess "11th Computer Olympiad") <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> and the [12th Computer Olympiad, Amsterdam 2007](12th_Computer_Olympiad#ChineseChess "12th Computer Olympiad").
