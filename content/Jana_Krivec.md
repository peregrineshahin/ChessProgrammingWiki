[ Jana Krivec <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>
**Jana Krivec**,  

a Slovenian [chess woman grandmaster](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woman_Grandmaster#Woman_Grandmaster_.28WGM.29), computer scientist and researcher at *Department of Intelligent systems* <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>, [Jožef Stefan Institute](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jo%C5%BEef_Stefan_Institute), [Ljubljana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ljubljana). 
Previously, she was affiliated with the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a> at [University of Ljubljana](University_of_Ljubljana "University of Ljubljana"). Her research interests covers [machine learning](Learning "Learning") and automated explanation / tutoring systems.
