 [](File:Sjakk.JPG) [Sjakk](http://no.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sjakkolympiaden_2014) i [Tromsø](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Troms%C3%B8) <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> 
**Sjakk**,  

a [WinBoard](WinBoard "WinBoard") and since version **2.0** [UCI](UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine by [Jacob Børs Lind](index.php?title=Jacob_B%C3%B8rs_Lind&action=edit&redlink=1 "Jacob Børs Lind (page does not exist)"). 
Sjakk was initially written in [Visual Basic 2010 Express](Basic#VB "Basic"), first released as WinBoard engine and also with an own [GUI](GUI "GUI") in July 2009. 
Since 2012, Sjakk was ported to [C++](Cpp "Cpp"), as Sjakk v2.0 released on April 01, 2013. Since version **2.1**, Sjakk uses a [power of two](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_of_two) number of entries inside its [transposition table](Transposition_Table "Transposition Table") with an adjustable [base 2 exponent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Exponentiation#Powers_of_two) > 0, which allows the faster index calculation replacing the [modulo operation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modulo_operation) by [bitwise AND](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modulo_operation#Performance_issues) <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
