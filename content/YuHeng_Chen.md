**Yu-Heng Chen**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, [National Taiwan Normal University](National_Taiwan_Normal_University "National Taiwan Normal University").
He works with [Shun-Shii Lin](Shun-Shii_Lin "Shun-Shii Lin") et al. on improvements in [Monte-Carlo Tree Search](Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") (MCTS),
adressing the average reward problem in sudden death games (such as [connection games](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Connection_game)) proposing a [Minimax](Minimax "Minimax") MCTS hybrid <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
