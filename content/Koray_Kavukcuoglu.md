 [](https://www.linkedin.com/in/koray-kavukcuoglu-0439a720) Koray Kavukcuoglu <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> 
*Koray Kavukcuoglu'*,  

a Turkish aerospace engineer, computer scientist, and researcher at [Google](index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1 "Google (page does not exist)") [DeepMind](index.php?title=DeepMind&action=edit&redlink=1 "DeepMind (page does not exist)"), involved in the [WaveNet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WaveNet) speech synthesis system, and the [AlphaGo](index.php?title=AlphaGo&action=edit&redlink=1 "AlphaGo (page does not exist)") project. He holds [degrees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Engineer's_degree#Turkey) in [Aerospace engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aerospace_engineering) from [Middle East Technical University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_East_Technical_University), [Ankara](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ankara), and M.Sc. and Ph.D. in computer science from [New York University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_University) in 2005 and 2010 respectively. His research interests focus on [machine learning](Learning "Learning") including [neural networks](Neural_Networks "Neural Networks") and [deep learning](Deep_Learning "Deep Learning"). Along with [Ronan Collobert](index.php?title=Ronan_Collobert&action=edit&redlink=1 "Ronan Collobert (page does not exist)") and [Clément Farabet](index.php?title=Cl%C3%A9ment_Farabet&action=edit&redlink=1 "Clément Farabet (page does not exist)"), he is developer of the version of the machine learning library Torch5 <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a> and also contributed to subsequent versions <a id="cite-note-4" href="#cite-ref-4">[4]</a>.
