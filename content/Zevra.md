**Zevra**,  

an [UCI](UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") by [Oleg Smirnov](index.php?title=Oleg_Smirnov&action=edit&redlink=1 "Oleg Smirnov (page does not exist)"), written in [C++](Cpp "Cpp") and licensed under the [GNU General Public License](Free_Software_Foundation#GPL "Free Software Foundation"). After a two years break, the development continued. **Zevra2** was released in August 2021 <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
