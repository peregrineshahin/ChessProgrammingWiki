**Akira Ura**,

a Japanese computer scientist and [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") programmer affiliated with the Graduate School of Engineering, [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo), and recent co-author of the strong Shogi engine [Gekisashi](index.php?title=Gekisashi&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gekisashi (page does not exist)"), with interests in [game learning](Learning "Learning"), [automated tuning](Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") and [parallel search](Parallel_Search "Parallel Search").
