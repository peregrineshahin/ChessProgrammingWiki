**Nobuo Araki**,  

a Japanese computer scientist, and M.Sc. in 2008 from School of Information Science and Technology, [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo), and Ph.D. student at [University of Electro-Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Electro-Communications) where his research on computer [Go](Go "Go") is supported by the [Japan Society for the Promotion of Science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan_Society_for_the_Promotion_of_Science). He is author of [MC\_ark](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=528), a Go playing program performing [MCTS](Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search"), inspired by [Rémi Coulom's](R%C3%A9mi_Coulom "Rémi Coulom") [CGW 2007](CGW_2007 "CGW 2007") paper *Computing Elo ratings of move patterns in the game of Go* <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>.
