**IEEE**,  

the [Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Institute_of_Electrical_and_Electronics_Engineers), a professional association headquartered in [New York City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City), dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence. IEEE produces 30% of the world's literature in the electrical and electronics engineering and computer science fields, publishing well over 100 [peer-reviewed journals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_journal) <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
