[ K2 <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>
**K2**,  

an [open source chess engine](Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") by [Sergey Meus](index.php?title=Sergey_Meus&action=edit&redlink=1 "Sergey Meus (page does not exist)"), written in [C++](Cpp "Cpp") and released under the [GPL v2.0](Free_Software_Foundation#GPL "Free Software Foundation"). 
K2 supports both [UCI](UCI "UCI") and the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol").
