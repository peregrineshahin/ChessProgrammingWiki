**LaGrande**,  

a chess program by [Gabriele Müller](Gabriele_M%C3%BCller "Gabriele Müller") as successor of her [Crafty](Crafty "Crafty") [clone](Category:Clone "Category:Clone") [Voyager](Voyager "Voyager"). 
Voyager, LaGrande and its "sister" [LaPetite](LaPetite "LaPetite") were promoted by [Frank Quisinsky](Frank_Quisinsky "Frank Quisinsky"), 
when he initially assumed Voyager an experimental private chess program by Gabriele Müller <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>, even after it was exposed as clone in February 1999 <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>, but later admitted the evidence of cloning <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>. LaGrande was the "sister" of the third clone LaPetite, which was according to Quisinsky a much more aggressive and speculative player <a id="cite-note-4" href="#cite-ref-4">[4]</a>.
