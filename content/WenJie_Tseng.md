 [](https://icga.org/?page_id=800&wppa-album=3&wppa-cover=0&wppa-occur=1&wppa-photo=45) Wen-Jie Tseng <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> 
**Wen-Jie Tseng**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, [National Chiao Tung University](National_Chiao_Tung_University "National Chiao Tung University"), [Hsinchu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hsinchu). He is co-author of the [Chinese Chess](Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess") engine [Chimo](index.php?title=Chimo&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chimo (page does not exist)"), and further co-author of two programs which won their respective tournaments at the [17th Computer Olympiad](17th_Computer_Olympiad "17th Computer Olympiad") in Yokohama 2013 , the [Chinese Dark Chess](Chinese_Dark_Chess "Chinese Dark Chess") engine *DarkKnight* <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> , and the [Connect6](Connect6 "Connect6") engine *Mobile6* <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a> .
