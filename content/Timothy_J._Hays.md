**Timothy J. (Tim) Hays**,  

an American software developer and lead programmer of the evolutionary [checkers](Checkers "Checkers") program [Blondie24](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blondie24). He worked along with [David B. Fogel](David_B._Fogel "David B. Fogel") et al. on evolutionary chess <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>, yielding to the chess program dubbed [Blondie25](Blondie25 "Blondie25") <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
