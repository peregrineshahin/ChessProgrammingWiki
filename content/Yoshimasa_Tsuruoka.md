**Yoshimasa Tsuruoka**,  

a Japanese computer scientist, [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") programmer and associate professor at [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo) and before at *School of Information Science* <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> , [Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST)](JAIST "JAIST"). His research topics include [natural language processing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_language_processing), [text mining](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Text_mining) from [biomedical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biomedical_engineering) literature, [artificial intelligence](Artificial_Intelligence "Artificial Intelligence") in [games](Games "Games"), and [deep learning](Deep_Learning "Deep Learning"). Yoshimasa Tsuruoka is co-author of the Shogi engine [Gekisashi](index.php?title=Gekisashi&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gekisashi (page does not exist)"), Gold medal winner of the [15th Computer Olympiad, Shogi](15th_Computer_Olympiad#Shogi "15th Computer Olympiad") in [Kanazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa,_Ishikawa).
