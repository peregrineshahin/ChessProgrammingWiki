**Xi Liang** (梁熙),  

a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science at [National Chiao Tung University](National_Chiao_Tung_University "National Chiao Tung University"), [Hsinchu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hsinchu). In 2015, he defended his master's degree in CS on the topic of solving [Hex](Hex "Hex") openings using Job-level [UCT](UCT "UCT") (JL-UCT) <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>, further published along with [Ting-Han Wei](Ting-Han_Wei "Ting-Han Wei") and [I-Chen Wu](I-Chen_Wu "I-Chen Wu") in the [ICGA Journal](ICGA_Journal#38_3 "ICGA Journal") <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
