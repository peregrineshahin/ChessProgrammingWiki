**Evangelos Karountzos**,

a Greece computer scientist affiliated with [University of Groningen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen) and currently working on [requirements traceability](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Requirements_traceability).
Along with [Zacharias Georgiou](Zacharias_Georgiou "Zacharias Georgiou"), [Yaroslav Shkarupa](Yaroslav_Shkarupa "Yaroslav Shkarupa") and [Matthia Sabatelli](Matthia_Sabatelli "Matthia Sabatelli"), he worked on a project dealing with [reinforcement learning](Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") in simple [chess endgames](Endgame "Endgame") such as [KRK](KRK "KRK") <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
