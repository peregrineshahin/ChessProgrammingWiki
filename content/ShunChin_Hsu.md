 [](http://www.iis.sinica.edu.tw/Conference/ICGA2005/icga/img/2005_09_04/slides/IMG_0264.html) Shun-Chin Hsu <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a> 
**Shun-Chin Hsu**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientiest and Professor at the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> at the [National Taiwan University](National_Taiwan_University "National Taiwan University"). His research interests covers [Games](Games "Games"), specially [Chinese Chess](Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess") and [Computer Go](Go "Go"), [Retrograde Analysis](Retrograde_Analysis "Retrograde Analysis") for Chinese Chess [Endgame Tablebases](Endgame_Tablebases "Endgame Tablebases"), and various [search](Search "Search") issues. Shun-Chin Hsu is co-author of various strong programs, successfully competing at [ICGA](ICGA "ICGA") Tournaments <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a> of [Chinese Chess](Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess"), [Go](Go "Go") and [Connect6](Connect6 "Connect6"). Along with [Shi-Jim Yen](Shi-Jim_Yen "Shi-Jim Yen") and [Tsan-Cheng Su](index.php?title=Tsan-Cheng_Su&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tsan-Cheng Su (page does not exist)"), Shun-Chin Hsu is author of the [Othello](Othello "Othello") program *Tower of Othello*, which won silver at [TAAI 2013](index.php?title=TAAI_2013&action=edit&redlink=1 "TAAI 2013 (page does not exist)") <a id="cite-note-4" href="#cite-ref-4">[4]</a> and the gold medal (shared) at [TCGA 2014](index.php?title=TCGA_2014&action=edit&redlink=1 "TCGA 2014 (page does not exist)").
