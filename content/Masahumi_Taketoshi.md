**Masahumi Taketoshi**, (Masafumi Taketoshi <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>)  

a Japanese computer scientist, in 2002 affiliated with Departments of Computer Science, [Shizuoka University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shizuoka_University), [Hamamatsu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamamatsu,_Shizuoka). As [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") programmer he is co-author of [Tacos](index.php?title=Tacos&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tacos (page does not exist)") <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>, the four times Gold medal winner at [Computer Olympiads](Computer_Olympiad "Computer Olympiad").
