**Shay Bushinsky**,  

an Israeli computer scientist and chess programmer. Together with [Amir Ban](Amir_Ban "Amir Ban"), Shay Bushinsky is author of the world class chess engine and multiple world champion [Junior](Junior "Junior"). He is researcher with the [CRI Institute](http://www.cri.haifa.ac.il/) [University of Haifa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Haifa). His research includes [opponent modeling search](Opponent_Model_Search "Opponent Model Search") as well as [evaluation function tuning](Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") with [supervised learning](Supervised_Learning "Supervised Learning") methods <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a> .
