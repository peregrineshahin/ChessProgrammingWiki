**Jun Okabe**, (岡部淳)  

a Japanese computer scientist, and computer [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") programmer. He was affiliated with the *Graduate School of Information Science and Technology,* 
[The University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo),
where he worked on instruction steering algorithms to improve the a balance of [clock rate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clock_rate) and [IPC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Instructions_per_cycle) of [microprocessors](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microprocessor) <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
