**Makoto Miwa**,  

a Japanese computer scientist and associate professor at [Toyota Technological Institute](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toyota_Technological_Institute), previously affiliated with the [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo). His research interests include [natural language processing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_language_processing), [game learning](Learning "Learning"), [automated tuning](Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") and [Upper Confidence bounds applied to Trees (UCT)](UCT "UCT").
