**Karen Simonyan**,  

a British computer scientist and researcher at [Google](index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1 "Google (page does not exist)") [DeepMind](index.php?title=DeepMind&action=edit&redlink=1 "DeepMind (page does not exist)"), involved in the [WaveNet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WaveNet) speech synthesis system, and in the [AlphaGo Zero](index.php?title=AlphaGo&action=edit&redlink=1 "AlphaGo (page does not exist)") and [AlphaZero](AlphaZero "AlphaZero") projects. He received his Ph.D. in [computer vision](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_vision) from [Oxford University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Oxford) in 2013 <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>, and was one of the founders of *Vision Factory*, an Oxford University [spin-off](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_spin-off), where his aim was to improve [visual recognition systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visual_perception) using [deep learning](Deep_Learning "Deep Learning"). *Vision Factory* was acquired by Google DeepMind in October 2014 <a id="cite-note-3" href="#cite-ref-3">[3]</a>.
