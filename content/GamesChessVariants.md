**[Home](Home "Home") * Games**

[John von Neumann](John_von_Neumann "John von Neumann") classified [Chess](Chess "Chess") as [two-player](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Two-player_game) [zero-sum](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero-sum_(game_theory)>) [abstract strategy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abstract_strategy) [board game](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Board_game) with [perfect information](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perfect_information). This page gives a brief overview on chess variants and other games, which are interesting for chess programmers due to new and innovative approaches and [algorithms](Algorithms "Algorithms") in [search](Search "Search"), [evaluation](Evaluation "Evaluation") and [learning](Learning "Learning").
