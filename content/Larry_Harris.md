**Larry R. Harris**,  

an American computer scientist and retired commercial [AI](Artificial_Intelligence "Artificial Intelligence") entrepreneur. He holds a Ph.D. in computer science in 1972 from [Cornell University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornell_University).



## AI Entrepreneur


Larry Harris further research interests were related to [natural language query systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural-language_processing), and he founded *Artificial Intelligence Corporation*, later renamed *AICorp*, which went public in 1990. In 1994 he founded the *EasyAsk* <a id="cite-note-4" href="#cite-ref-4">[4]</a> Linguistic Technology Corporation, and developed its *EasyAsk* and *English Wizard* [E-commerce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E-commerce) products.
