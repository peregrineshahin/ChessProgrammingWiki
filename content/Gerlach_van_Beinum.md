**Gerlach van Beinum**,

a Dutch computer scientist and former computer chess programmer. While affiliated with [Delft Institute of Technology](Delft_University_of_Technology "Delft University of Technology") in the early 80s, he was member of the [Pion](Pion "Pion") team along with [Sito Dekker](Sito_Dekker "Sito Dekker"), [Roger Hünen](Roger_H%C3%BCnen "Roger Hünen"), [Jan Derksen](Jan_Derksen "Jan Derksen"), [Harry Nefkens](Harry_Nefkens "Harry Nefkens") and [Jaap van den Herik](Jaap_van_den_Herik "Jaap van den Herik") <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
