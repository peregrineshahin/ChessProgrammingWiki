**Shield**,  

an [UCI](UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine by [Luigi Marco Ripamonti](Luigi_Marco_Ripamonti "Luigi Marco Ripamonti"), written in the [D programming language](D_(Programming_Language) "D (Programming Language)"). 
After [Tamerlane](Tamerlane "Tamerlane") and [Madeleine](Madeleine "Madeleine"), Shield is the third chess engine by Luigi Marco Ripamonti - its development started around November 2015. 
Shield had its tournament debut at the [IGWT IV](IGWT_IV "IGWT IV") in early 2017, where it qualified for the final steps, and lost in the round of sixteen versus later winner [Andscacs](Andscacs "Andscacs").
