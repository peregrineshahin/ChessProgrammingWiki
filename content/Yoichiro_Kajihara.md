**Yoichiro Kajihara**,  

a Japanese [Shogi](Shogi "Shogi") player and computer scientist at [Shizuoka University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shizuoka_University), [Hamamatsu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamamatsu,_Shizuoka). As a game programmer, Yoichiro Kajihara is author of the [Amazons](Amazons "Amazons") program [Aska](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=167), and member of team in developing the Shogi program [Tacos](index.php?title=Tacos&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tacos (page does not exist)") <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>.
