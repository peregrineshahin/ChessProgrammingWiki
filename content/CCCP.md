**CCCP** (Comparatively Complex Chess Program),

a closed source chess engine written by [Pawel Koziol](Pawel_Koziol "Pawel Koziol"), initially based on [TSCP](TSCP "TSCP"). Some ideas used in [evaluation function](Evaluation_Function "Evaluation Function") of the [CPW-Engine](CPW-Engine "CPW-Engine") are derived from it.
There is also a version of CCCP playing a [knightmate](Knightmate_Chess "Knightmate Chess") [chess variant](Games#ChessVariants "Games").

## Namesake

- [CCCP](</CCCP_(US)> "CCCP (US)") the Columbia Computer Chess Program
