The **8th International Computers and Games Conference**, [Keio University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keio_University) [Kyōsei-kan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Campus_of_Keio_University#Hiyoshi_campus), the Collaboration Complex <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>, [Yokohama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yokohama), [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan), August 13-15, [2013](Timeline#2013 "Timeline"). CG 2013 was held in conjunction with the [WCCC 2013](WCCC_2013 "WCCC 2013"), [WCSC 2013](WCSC_2013 "WCSC 2013"), and the [17th Computer Olympiad](17th_Computer_Olympiad "17th Computer Olympiad").
