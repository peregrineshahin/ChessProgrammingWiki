\[ Hungarian hussar <a id="cite-note-1" href="#cite-ref-1">[1]</a>
**Hussar**,

an [UCI](UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine by [Péter Horváth](P%C3%A9ter_Horv%C3%A1th "Péter Horváth").
Its development apparently started in 2009, after Péter's former engine [Asterisk](Asterisk "Asterisk") played the [MASPV 2009](MASPV_2009 "MASPV 2009").
Hussar **0.1** was first released in 2010.
Last recent version was Hussar **0.4**, released in 2011. Hussar is able to play [Chess960](Chess960 "Chess960") and supports [skill](Playing_Strength "Playing Strength") levels <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
