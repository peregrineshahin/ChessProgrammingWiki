**Intelligent Chess Software** (Intelligent Software),  

a series of experimental chess programs by [Intelligent Software](Intelligent_Software "Intelligent Software"), the software forge by [David Levy](David_Levy "David Levy") and [Kevin O’Connell](Kevin_O%E2%80%99Connell "Kevin O’Connell"), initially based on [Richard Lang's](Richard_Lang "Richard Lang") [Cyrus](Cyrus "Cyrus") program, which was also incorporated inside the [dedicated computers](Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers") [Chess 2001](Chess_2001 "Chess 2001") and [La Regence](La_Regence "La Regence").
