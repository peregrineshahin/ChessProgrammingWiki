**Jean-Baptiste Hoock**,  

 a French computer scientist and engineer. He received his M.Sc. in computer science and [image processing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image_processing) from [Caen University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Caen_Lower_Normandy) and engineering diploma from the [ENSICAEN Engineer School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89cole_nationale_sup%C3%A9rieure_d%27ing%C3%A9nieurs_de_Caen), [Caen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caen), [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France). He is working as engineer at [Paris-Sud University](University_of_Paris#11 "University of Paris").
