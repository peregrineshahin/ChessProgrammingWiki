**Chih-Wen (Steven) Hsueh**,

a Taiwanese computer scientist and associate professor in the *Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia* and 'Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering *at the [National Taiwan University](National_Taiwan_University "National Taiwan University").*
He received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in CS from the [University of Southern California](University_of_Southern_California "University of Southern California") and [University of California at Irvine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_California,_Irvine) in 1994 and 1997 respectively.
His research centers around [real-time computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real-time_computing) in particular [peal-time operating systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real-time_operating_system), [embedded systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embedded_system), and intelligent systems <a id="cite-note-2" href="#cite-ref-2">[2]</a>.
