**Yen-Chi Chen**,  

a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the [National Taiwan Normal University](National_Taiwan_Normal_University "National Taiwan Normal University").
His research interests include [machine learning](Learning "Learning") and [computer games](Games "Games"). He is author and co-author of [Chinese Dark Chess](Chinese_Dark_Chess "Chinese Dark Chess"), [Surakarta](index.php?title=Surakarta&action=edit&redlink=1 "Surakarta (page does not exist)"), [Outer-Open Gomoku](index.php?title=Outer-Open_Gomoku&action=edit&redlink=1 "Outer-Open Gomoku (page does not exist)"), [Othello](Othello "Othello") and [Nonogram](Nonogram "Nonogram") playing and solving programs, participating and winning at [ICGA](ICGA "ICGA") and [TAAI](index.php?title=TAAI&action=edit&redlink=1 "TAAI (page does not exist)") tournaments.
